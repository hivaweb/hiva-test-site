Admissions and Programs

Passing score

The passing score required for university admission at Ala-Too International University is one hundred and ten (110) points for the main test.

Internal tests

Ala-Too International University conducts an internal test in English to determine the level of language proficiency.

University transfers

Students can transfer, but with the exception of those who are in their first and last year at their current university. Additionally, you would typically need to provide an academic transcript from your current institution when applying for a transfer.
